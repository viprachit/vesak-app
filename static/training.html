Training Portal Global Standard SOPs

Operator Guidelines

Please review these standards strictly before attending client calls.

1

Inquiry Intake

Never say 'I don't know' immediately. If unsure, always say: 'Let me confirm this with my senior manager and get back to you.' Ask for the patient's age, medical history, and location immediately.

2

Rate Quoting & Negotiation

Always state the highest calculated 'Max Rate' (+15%) first. Only drop down to the 'Min Rate' (+8%) if the client attempts to dispute or negotiate. Do NOT quote below Min Rate without Super Admin approval.

3

Confirming the Service

Emphasize that the first invoice must be generated immediately upon assignment. No staff dispatch happens before the invoice is created and initial assurance is given by the client.
